Barquisimeto, The Twilight's City

Barquisimeto is the capital of the State of Lara, located in the Central-Western Region of Venezuela. It is known as the “City of Twilights” due to its impressive sunsets. Additionally, it is widely recognized as the “Musical Capital of Venezuela” due to the large number of musicians and cultural expressions it hosts.
